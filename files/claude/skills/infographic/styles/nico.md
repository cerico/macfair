# Style: nico

inherits: design:nico

Dark forest aesthetic with elegant restraint. Maps the nico design system tokens to infographic elements.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Literata, Georgia, serif — light weight (300), tight tracking
- **Body:** Literata, Georgia, serif — regular weight (400), line-height 1.7
- **Stats:** Large serif numbers (Literata/Georgia, 48px) — light weight (300), luminous against dark
- **Labels/meta:** Literata serif, letter-spacing: 3px, uppercase, 11px

## Color Palette

Dark, desaturated, luminous:
- Canvas: #1a2e1a (deep forest green-black)
- Text primary: #f5f2eb (warm off-white — glows against dark)
- Text secondary: #a8a090 (muted tan)
- Accent 1: #c4a35a (desaturated gold)
- Accent 2: #5a8a9a (desaturated teal)
- Accent 3: #a8a090 (muted tan, reused for subtlety)
- Rule lines: #3a4d3a (lighter forest)
- Highlight bg: #2a3d2a (secondary surface)

## Layout
- 800x1100px canvas
- 80px margins on all sides (generous whitespace)
- Content width: 640px
- Same vertical rhythm as c82 but with 56px section gap (more breathing room)

## Visual Elements
- **Border frame:** 1px rule line (#3a4d3a) inset 28px from edges
- **Section dividers:** Thin horizontal rules (0.5px, #3a4d3a)
- **Stats:** Light-weight serif numbers, luminous against dark canvas
- **Header:** Title centered, subtitle in italic serif, warm off-white
- **Footer/takeaway:** Double rule lines (#3a4d3a), italic text in off-white

## Philosophy
- Content should feel luminous against darkness
- Maximum 2-3 colors beyond neutrals
- Accents desaturated (30-45% saturation)
- Elegant restraint over decoration
